State University of New York at Oneonta

Oneonta Student Fashion Society's purpose is to help our fashion program and community gain a better understanding of the fashion industry. We focus on fashion production, event planning, public relations & advertising, modeling, and management through a series of events, meetings, field trips, activities, and many more fashion-related topics for fashion lovers of all backgrounds, experience, and goals.
